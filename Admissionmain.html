    Things To Know First

    The Common Application is required for students applying to any or all
    of SU’s three degree. You’ll be able to choose your campus and programs
    that you are interested.

    You will need :

    1. Contact information for the counselor or other school representative
    who will complete your Common Application School Report and
    submit your official high school transcript.

    2. Contact information for one teacher (or two, maximum) who will
    complete the Teacher Evaluation form.

    3. Nonrefundable $50 application fee. Students who are unable to pay
    the application fee can request a fee waiver.
